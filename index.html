Honest answers

Questions we get
before the deposit clears.

Is the walkthrough actually free? What's the catch?

Free, no catch. We drive out, walk the property with you, take measurements, and email you a written quote within 24 hours — no deposit, no consultation fee, no obligation. If we're a fit, great. If not, you've still got a quote and a plan. The only thing we ask is that you be home when we said we'd be there.

What's the deposit and when do I pay it?

50% of the total project cost, due before we order any material. It locks your spot on the calendar — we don't hold dates on a handshake, because material has gone up 30% in three years and we can't afford to. The remaining 50% is due the day the install is finished. Not before. Square handles payment, in person or by link.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

No. We've watched too many small contractors get burned by financing partners that take 8–12% off the top and leave the homeowner blaming us when payments come due. We keep our prices honest by skipping the middleman. Most banks, credit unions, and home equity lines beat any contractor financing program anyway.

What about HOA approval?

HOA paperwork and submittal is the customer's responsibility — you know your board better than we ever will, and they'll want it from the homeowner directly. We'll happily provide spec sheets, drawings, color samples, and anything else they ask for to support your packet. We've worked with most of the big Omaha HOAs and know what flies and what doesn't.

What if you hit my sprinklers?

If they're marked and we hit one, we repair it on our dime. If they're not marked, the homeowner covers the repair. Mark your lines before we dig — paint cans are cheap insurance.

Do you work in winter?

Residential when the weather and soil cooperate — we're not setting posts in frozen mud. Commercial year-round; we use a skid steer that punches through frost. If you have a hard deadline (closing date, insurance inspection), call us and we'll figure it out together.

Where do you serve?

Omaha, Lincoln, Gretna, Fremont, Norfolk, Papillion, Bellevue, Elkhorn, and most of eastern Nebraska. If you're outside that radius, call us anyway — we travel for the right project.
